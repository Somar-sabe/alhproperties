About the project

LIV LUX is the crown jewel of the Dubai Marina community. The development offers refined luxury with breathtaking views of the Arabian sea, Dubai Marina and Palm Jumeirah. Residents of the waterfront complex will enjoy living near the beach.

Units in the residential complex are designed with maximum efficiency in mind, offering more usable space for residents. LIV LUX takes its inspiration from the very lifestyle it creates – enjoy stunning ocean views from the serenity of your own private balcony. At the current moment the residential project is under development with the anticipated date of handover set to be December 2026.

Residence features

Properties in the complex are presented by exquisite residences with 1 and 2 bedrooms as well as signature apartments with 2, 3 and 4 bedrooms. Apartments feature a minimum of 10 ft ceiling heights, fantastic open-air balconies and floor to ceiling windows allowing for natural sunlight.

LIV LUX also offers a limited collection of half floor duplex penthouses ranging from 7,250 to 15,250 sq. ft. Exclusive homes are distinguished by infinity swimming pools, entertainment spaces and stunning skyline views. There is also The LIV Superlux Penthouse, which offers residents luxury of another level with outdoor entertainment spaces, a private Art Gallery and terraces with Zen Gardens, private massage areas, outdoor jacuzzi and sun-lounging area.

Each expansive residence features flowing, open concept floor plans. Luxurious units in LIV LUX feature fully fitted premium European kitchens with the latest innovations. Thoughtfully planned bedrooms in properties are accompanied by stunning waterfront views, well curated wardrobes, storage and shelving ensure that both style and function coexist. Luxurious bathrooms are fitted with the finest in European Brands such as Villeroy &amp; Boch.

AMENITIES

One of the main features of the residential building is that the entire 7th floor of LIV LUX is allocated to premium indoor and outdoor amenities allowing for resort style living.

It includes an infinity swimming pool, Golf Putting Green, outdoor BBQ & dining areas, outdoor gardens, indoor movie theater, outdoor cinema, yoga studio, fitness center, well equipped conference rooms and much more. LIV LUX offers retreat into the dedicated spa with sauna and steam rooms, with changing facilities for ladies and gents. There is also a Private Salon available to residents only and a luxury Residents Lounge for entertainment and private events.

Spacious grand double height lobby with perfect details in the building welcomes residents and their guests. There is the Reception, manned with round-the-clock security, concierge and guest services.

IN THE SOUGHT-AFTER AREA OF DUBAI MARINA

Location

The residential complex of LIV LUX has a prestigious location in the sought-after area of Dubai Marina, which is well-known for its white-sand beach and entertainment attractions. The development is close to Sheikh Zayed Road, which provides a fast connectivity to other parts of the emirate of Dubai. Neighboring districts include Dubai Media City, Jumeirah Lake Towers and Emirates Hills. The travel time to Al Maktoum International Airport takes a 35-minute drive, while Dubai International Airport can be reached in a 45-minute drive.

The LIV LUX complex is surrounded by 5-star hotels such as Le Royal Meridien Beach Resort & Spa, Ritz Carlton and InterContinental Dubai Marina.

Residents of LIV LUX can easily reach various dining, leisure and shopping destinations including Emirates Golf Club & TopGolf Dubai, Dubai Marina Mall, Ain Dubai on Bluewaters Island as well as Montgomerie Golf Club in Emirates Hills. The road to all above-mentioned facilities will take about a 5-minute drive. Spacious grand double height lobby with perfect details in the building welcomes residents and their guests. There is the Reception, manned with round-the-clock security, concierge and guest services.
